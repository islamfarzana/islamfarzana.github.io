Luxury Room

Delux Family Rooms

Step into luxury with our Single Room at Capital Paradise Hotel. Designed for the discerning traveler seeking comfort and convenience, this room offers a perfect retreat amidst the city buzz.

Upon entering, you'll be greeted by a serene ambiance adorned with modern furnishings and tasteful decor. Sink into the plush comfort of your cozy bed, adorned with crisp linens and fluffy pillows, promising a restful night's sleep.

Stay connected with complimentary high-speed internet access, perfect for catching up on work or staying in touch with loved ones. Unwind in front of the flat-screen television, offering a variety of entertainment options for your relaxation.

Refresh in the elegant ensuite bathroom, equipped with premium toiletries and plush towels, ensuring a rejuvenating experience after a day of exploration.
